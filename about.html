ABOUT THE GARDEN

The Persistence Garden is an explorable 2D world celebrating patterns, persistence, and the L20 Rogue achievement. It is an expression of the incremental grinder archetype - a philosophy of battle-by-battle linear progression and patience-based optimization.

By reading intent, mapping patterns, and trusting the math of persistence, the Rogue turns repetition into resilience and the long arc of the journey bends toward mastery.
